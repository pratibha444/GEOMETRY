 In 60 minutes minute hand covers  360$\degree$\\
For 5 minutes 6$\degree$ $\times$ 5 = 30$\degree$\\ 
Here $\theta$ = 30 $\degree$ and r = 14cm\\

\begin{align*}
	\text{Area of sector} = \frac{\theta}{360} \times \pi r^2
	=51.31cm^2.
\end{align*}
